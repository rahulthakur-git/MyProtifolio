Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Rossi Family bella vista has been authentic italian cuisine for over three decades. Our passion for traditional recipes and fresh ingredients has been made us a beloved destination for food lover

Every dish is prepered with love and attention to detail, using recipes passed down thorugh generations of italian chefs
